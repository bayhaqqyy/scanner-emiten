Rule Ringkas

Scalping

  • Transaksi >= 5B (nilai transaksi)
  • Harga > VWAP dan VWAP naik
  • EMA9 > EMA21 dan gap melebar
  • RSI 50 sampai 70
  • Volume entry >= 1.3x
  • Break high minor
  • 2 candle green solid body

Swing

  • Harga > EMA20 & EMA20 > EMA50
  • EMA20 slope naik
  • RSI 45 sampai 65
  • Volume breakout >= 1.2x
  • Break resistance + close kuat
  • Body solid, tanpa upper shadow panjang

BSJP

  • Volume >= 1.5x MA20
  • Value > 10B
  • Return harian > 0.5%

BPJS

  • Close >= Prev Close
  • Close >= Open
  • Value > 2B
